# Business Plan

## Executive Summary
Echo is a personal budgeting app for individuals and small friend groups who want clean monthly planning, clear cashflow, and simple account-based privacy.

## Problem
Most budgeting apps are either too complex, too expensive, or too opinionated for casual personal use.

## Solution
Echo delivers:
- Fast budget setup by month and folder
- Clear timeline of income and expenses
- Shared product consistency with private user data
- Practical planning tools without feature overload

## Target Customer
- Primary: individuals age 20-45 managing monthly expenses
- Secondary: friend groups who each want private accounts in one app

## Value Proposition
- Easier than spreadsheet budgeting
- Cleaner than enterprise-style finance apps
- Privacy-first by default (user-scoped data)

## Monetization (Phase-Based)
- Phase 1: free beta for adoption and feedback
- Phase 2: optional paid tier for advanced reporting and automation
- Phase 3: team/household collaboration add-on

## Go-To-Market
- Launch with personal network and social proof screenshots
- Gather top 10 pain points from first active users
- Weekly release rhythm with visible changelog

## 12-Month Business Goals
- 1,000 registered users
- 300 monthly active users
- 30% month-2 retention
- 99.5% uptime

## Budget and Cost Control
- Hosting: Render starter/free during beta
- Database/Auth: Supabase
- Keep infrastructure simple until user/usage growth requires scaling

## Immediate Next 30 Days
- Stabilize deploy and auth flows
- Ship sprint process and QA gates
- Execute user-requested improvements one-by-one
